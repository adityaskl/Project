About Pind

A web app to help users and the government prepare for and reallocate resources for natural disasters.

Floods and droughts are frequent and dangerous natural disasters. Floods inundate areas with water, while droughts cause extended periods of water scarcity.

Every year, floods claim thousands of lives, displacing millions. In India and China, the Yellow River Valley witnesses devastating floods. Droughts, exacerbated by climate change and poor water management, devastate agriculture, leading to food insecurity and economic hardships for millions globally.

Our team wanted to help solve this problem, so we created Pind- An Intelligent Natural Disaster Predictor to predict floods and droughts and their impact before they even happen. Then, we display this information in an interactive graphical format, making the information compelling and easy to understand for people and governments alike.

Services

Our website offers many services to help people plan and prepare for floods and droughts, and help the government reallocate resources to those most in need.

View Plots

On our website, you can view plots showing flood prediction, droughts prediction, precipitation analysis, and damage analysis.

View Density maps

Our website also displays interactive Density maps sharing various realtime data on floods and droughts.

Predict Floods and Droughts

Lastly, our website allows you to predict floods and droughts in realtime through our machine learning algorithm.
